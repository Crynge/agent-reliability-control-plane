# Ideal Customer Profile

## Primary buyer

- SaaS and internal platform teams running 2-20 agent workflows
- teams already feeling production pain, not still debating whether to use agents

## Strong wedge

- internal automation and SaaS operations agents

These teams tend to have:

- measurable completion rates
- expensive failures
- manual operators already stepping in
- tool-heavy workflows that break in obvious, debuggable ways

## Core objections

- “We already have logs.”
- “Our framework says it handles state.”
- “We do not want another platform.”

## Counter-positioning

- logs are not replayable checkpoints
- frameworks do not guarantee durable recovery across your real tools
- the control plane complements existing orchestration rather than replacing it
